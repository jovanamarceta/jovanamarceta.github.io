About FitnessPro Gym

At FitnessPro Gym we are passionate about Fitness & Health, therefore we have created a top of the line facility which boasts the latest in progressive resistance, cardio-vascular conditioning, circuit training and free weight equipment.

FitnessPro Gym is all about training, making it a part of your daily lifestyle, as well as creating and maintaining an optimistic state of mind. Strength training, functional training and group training classes are what people want and what this Miami gym delivers..
